Research interests

I am broadly interested in the interface of geometry and physics.

My research often revolves around the moduli spaces of Higgs bundles and holomorphic/projective
connections on Riemann surfaces, in particular the integrable structure and stratification on these moduli
spaces. My motivation, in addition, comes from the approaches to the geometric Langlands program using
techniques from conformal field theories, such as the one pioneered by Beilinson-Drinfeld in which the
quantization of the moduli of Higgs bundles plays a key role.

Recently, I am also interested in random geometry, as well as its relation to enumerative geometry and
conformal field theories.
